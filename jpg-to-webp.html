About JPG to WebP

WebP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. By converting your standard JPG (or JPEG) files to WebP, you can reduce file sizes by up to 30% or more without a noticeable loss in quality. This leads to faster loading times for websites and a better user experience.

Our converter utilizes Client-Side Rendering (CSR) technology. This means that when you "upload" a file, it is actually being processed by your own computer's browser. There are no servers involved in the conversion, ensuring that your private photos and graphics stay strictly local.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is WebP better than JPG?

For web use, generally yes. WebP files are smaller than JPGs of similar quality, which improves SEO and site speed.
