About PPT to PDF

PowerPoint is great for presenting, but PDF is the universal standard for handouts and digital distribution. Converting PPTX to PDF ensures that your fonts, animations-frames, and layouts are "frozen" exactly as you designed them, making your file readable on any device without needing Office software.

Our tool runs entirely on the Client-Side. By utilizing modern JavaScript libraries, your browser handles the slide decompression and PDF rendering locally. Your sensitive pitch decks and company presentations are never uploaded to a server.

How to Convert PPT to PDF

  1. Upload: Select your .pptx files by dragging them into the drop zone or clicking the icon.
  2. Review: You can upload multiple presentations at once for batch processing.
  3. Convert: Click "CONVERT TO PDF". The tool will process each slide and compile them into a PDF document.
  4. Download: Your high-quality PDF files will download to your computer automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. The entire process occurs in your browser. We never see, store, or transmit your presentation data to any remote server.

Does it support .ppt (older version)?

This tool is optimized for the modern .pptx format. If you have an older .ppt file, we recommend saving it as .pptx in PowerPoint before using this tool.

Will my animations be in the PDF?

PDF is a static format. Animations and transitions will not be active, but all slide content will be rendered clearly on individual pages.

Is there a slide limit?

There is no slide limit, though very large presentations with high-resolution images may take longer to process depending on your computer's speed.
