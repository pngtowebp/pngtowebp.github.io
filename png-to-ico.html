About PNG to ICO

PNG is a versatile image format, especially for web graphics with transparency. However, for favicons or Windows desktop icons, the ICO format is specifically required. Our PNG to ICO converter makes it easy to take your high-quality PNGs and generate standard .ico files, including multiple resolutions within a single file for optimal display across different devices.

This tool operates with Client-Side Rendering (CSR). Your images are processed entirely within your web browser's memory, utilizing JavaScript to handle the conversion. This guarantees that your original artwork and converted icons are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring your privacy and data security.

How to Convert PNG to ICO

  1. Upload PNG: Drag your .png files into the drop zone, or click to select them from your computer.
  2. Review Files: Ensure your PNGs are listed correctly. You can convert multiple images in one go.
  3. Convert: Click "CONVERT TO ICO". The tool will generate different sizes (16x16, 32x32, 48x48, 256x256) from your source PNG.
  4. Download: Your new .ico files will be automatically downloaded, ready for use as favicons or desktop icons.

Frequently Asked Questions

What icon sizes are generated?

By default, the converter generates popular sizes like 16x16, 32x32, 48x48, and 256x256 pixels to ensure your icon looks sharp on various platforms and displays.

Is transparency maintained?

Yes. The PNG format's alpha channel transparency is fully preserved when converting to ICO, ensuring your icons have transparent backgrounds.

Is my image data secure?

Absolutely. All processing occurs locally in your browser. Your PNG images and generated ICOs are never uploaded to our servers.

What if my PNG is not square?

If your PNG is not square, the tool will typically center the image and pad the remaining space to fit the square ICO dimensions without distortion.
