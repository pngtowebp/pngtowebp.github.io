Why Convert GIF to WebP?

WebP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. By converting your GIFs to WebP, you can significantly reduce file sizes, which leads to faster page load times and reduced bandwidth usage.

Benefits of WebP over GIF

  • Better Compression: WebP files are typically 25-34% smaller than equivalent GIFs.
  • Rich Color Support: WebP supports 24-bit RGB color with an 8-bit alpha channel, compared to GIF's limited 8-bit color palette.
  • Faster Loading: Smaller file sizes mean your website or app loads much faster for users.
  • Browser Support: WebP is supported by all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari.
