Why Rasterize SVG to PNG?

SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) is perfect for logos and icons because it doesn't lose quality when resized. However, many applications, social media platforms, and legacy systems require raster formats like PNG.

Key Use Cases

  • Universal Viewing: PNG files can be viewed on any device without specialized software.
  • Social Media: Most platforms allow PNG uploads but do not support direct SVG uploads.
  • Office Documents: Easily insert your vector designs into Word, PowerPoint, or Excel as standard images.
  • Transparency: Like SVG, PNG supports transparent backgrounds, making it the perfect raster alternative.
