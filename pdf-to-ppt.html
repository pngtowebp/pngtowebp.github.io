About PDF to PPT

PDFs are excellent for distributing static documents, but they aren't ideal for live presentations. By converting a PDF to PowerPoint format, you can easily integrate your documents into a larger slide deck or use standard presentation tools like a clicker and presenter notes.

Our tool uses Client-Side Rendering (CSR). Using Mozilla's PDF.js to render pages and PptxGenJS to package them, the entire process happens in your browser. This means your confidential reports or portfolio PDFs never touch a remote server, ensuring total privacy.

How to Convert PDF to PPT

  1. Upload: Select your PDF files by dragging them into the tool or clicking to browse.
  2. Verify: Review the documents in your list. You can convert multiple PDFs at once.
  3. Process: Click "CONVERT TO PPT". The browser will render each PDF page into a slide layout.
  4. Save: Your new PowerPoint (.pptx) file will be generated and downloaded instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I edit the text in PowerPoint?

This converter renders PDF pages as high-fidelity images onto slides to ensure perfect layout preservation. While the text itself isn't editable as a text box, the slide quality is professional and clear.

Is it safe for work documents?

Yes. Since the conversion happens entirely on your local machine, no data is ever sent to our servers. It is 100% private and secure.

Does it support password-protected PDFs?

Currently, you must remove the password from your PDF before uploading it to the tool for conversion.

Will the slide size match the PDF?

The tool automatically adjusts slide dimensions to match the orientation and aspect ratio of your PDF pages.
