About PSD to PNG

PSD files are the industry standard for graphic design, containing multiple layers, masks, and adjustment data. However, they are large and difficult to view without Adobe Photoshop. Converting your PSD to PNG flattens these layers into a highly compatible web format while preserving the transparency and color depth of your design.

Our tool uses Client-Side Rendering (CSR). By using a specialized JavaScript parser, we decode the PSD binary data directly in your browser. This is essential for professional designers who handle sensitive or proprietary client work, as the original file is never transmitted to an external server.

How to Convert PSD to PNG

  1. Upload PSD: Drag your .psd files into the converter or click the upload area to select them.
  2. Process: Once your files are in the queue, click "CONVERT TO PNG".
  3. Flattening: The tool reads the composite image data from the PSD file and renders it onto a canvas.
  4. Save: The resulting PNG image will be downloaded to your computer automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I convert PSD files without Photoshop?

Yes. This tool functions as a standalone PSD viewer and converter. You do not need to have Adobe Creative Cloud installed on your machine.

Are the layers preserved?

To create a PNG image, the tool flattens all visible layers into a single image. It does not export individual layers as separate files.

Is there a limit on file size?

Since the decoding happens in your browser's RAM, very large PSD files (e.g., several gigabytes) may cause the browser to slow down. For standard design files, it works perfectly.

Is my design data safe?

Absolutely. The "conversion" is a local process. Your PSD data is parsed and turned into a PNG entirely on your own device.
