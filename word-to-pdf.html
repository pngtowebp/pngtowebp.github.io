About Word to PDF

Word documents are ideal for editing, but PDF is the standard for sharing because it preserves formatting across all devices and operating systems. Converting DOCX to PDF ensures that your resumes, reports, and contracts look exactly as intended when opened by others.

This tool uses Client-Side Rendering (CSR). By leveraging JavaScript libraries like Mammoth.js, your browser parses the document structure and converts it locally. Unlike other online converters, your private documents are never uploaded to a cloud server.

How to Convert Word to PDF

  1. Upload: Drag your .docx files into the drop zone or click to select them from your computer.
  2. Verify: Review your documents in the list. You can add multiple files for batch processing.
  3. Process: Click "CONVERT TO PDF". The browser will analyze the document and generate a PDF version.
  4. Download: Your converted PDF files will be saved to your device instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my document data kept private?

Absolutely. The conversion happens entirely in your browser's memory. No document data is ever sent to our servers or stored anywhere.

Does it support older .doc files?

Currently, this tool supports the modern .docx format. For older .doc files, we recommend saving them as .docx in Word before converting.

Will my formatting be identical?

While we strive for high fidelity, complex layouts and certain proprietary Word fonts may vary slightly. For standard business documents, the results are excellent.

Can I convert multiple files at once?

Yes, you can upload and convert multiple Word documents in a single batch session.
