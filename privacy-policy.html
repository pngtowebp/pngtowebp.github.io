Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our online tools.

1. Client-Side Processing

The core of our service is Client-Side Rendering (CSR). When you use our converters (e.g., TIFF to JPG, PSD to PNG, or MP4 to WebM), the processing happens entirely within your browser using JavaScript and WebAssembly. Your files are never uploaded to our servers.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personal identification information. We do not store the images, videos, or documents you process. Any data used during conversion resides in your device's temporary memory (RAM) and is cleared when you close the browser tab.

3. Cookies and Analytics

We may use basic cookies or third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) to understand website traffic and improve user experience. These tools collect non-personal data such as browser type and pages visited.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this policy, contact us at nolushac@gmail.com.
