About JPG to GIF Conversion

Our JPG to GIF converter is designed for users who need to transform standard photographs into the widely supported GIF format. While GIFs are often associated with animation, they are also an excellent format for simple graphics, logos with limited color palettes, and ensuring compatibility with older web legacy systems.

This tool uses Client-Side Rendering (CSR), meaning your files are never uploaded to a server. All the heavy lifting happens right in your browser, making it one of the most secure ways to convert images online.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my JPG become an animated GIF?

No, this tool converts static JPG images into static GIF files. It is primarily used for format compatibility and small graphic conversion.

Is there a limit on file size?

Since the conversion happens on your own computer, there are no artificial limits from our side. However, very large files may depend on your browser's available memory.

Are my images kept private?

Yes. Because we use client-side technology, your images are never sent to our servers. Your privacy is guaranteed.

Does the conversion affect image quality?

GIFs use a palette of up to 256 colors. For detailed photographs, you might notice some color simplification, which is a characteristic of the GIF format itself.
