About BMP to PNG

BMP (Bitmap) files are uncompressed and can be very large, making them less practical for web use or sharing. PNG (Portable Network Graphics) offers lossless compression, supporting transparency and a smaller file size without sacrificing image quality. It's the ideal format for logos, graphics, and images requiring a transparent background.

Our converter uses Client-Side Rendering (CSR). When you upload a BMP, your browser's powerful HTML5 Canvas API processes the image, converting it to PNG. This means your private images never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the conversion lossless?

Yes, PNG is a lossless format, so converting from BMP to PNG will not result in any degradation of image quality.

Does it support transparency?

Yes, PNG fully supports alpha channel transparency. If your BMP has an alpha channel, it will be preserved in the PNG output.

Are my images secure?

Absolutely. All image processing occurs locally in your browser. No files are uploaded to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

Is there a file size limit?

The practical limit is determined by your browser's memory. Large BMP files (e.g., extremely high resolution) may take longer to process, but the tool is designed for efficiency.
