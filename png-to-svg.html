About PNG to SVG

Our PNG to SVG converter is a powerful client-side tool designed to bridge the gap between raster and vector graphics. SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) is an XML-based image format that allows images to scale infinitely without losing quality. By converting your PNGs to SVG, you ensure they look sharp on everything from high-resolution retina displays to large format prints.

How to Convert PNG to SVG

  1. Upload: Click the drop zone or drag and drop your PNG files directly into the converter.
  2. Preview: View your selected files in the list. You can remove any unwanted files before processing.
  3. Convert: Click the "CONVERT TO SVG" button. The tool will process each file individually.
  4. Download: Your browser will automatically trigger the download for your new SVG files.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PNG to SVG converter free?

Yes! Our tool is 100% free to use with no limits on the number of files you can convert.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Because this is a Client-Side Rendered (CSR) tool, your images are processed locally in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

Can I convert multiple PNGs at once?

Yes, our converter supports batch processing. Simply select multiple files or drag them all at once into the upload area.

Does the SVG output maintain transparency?

Yes, the converter preserves the alpha channel (transparency) from your original PNG image in the final SVG output.
