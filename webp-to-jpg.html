Universal Compatibility with JPG

While WebP offers excellent compression, JPG remains the most universally supported image format in the world. Converting to JPG ensures your images will work on older devices, legacy software, and platforms that haven't yet adopted the WebP standard.

When to use JPG instead of WebP

  • Maximum Compatibility: Use JPG for email attachments or older web browsers.
  • Print Services: Most physical printing services specifically require JPG files.
  • Social Media: Some platforms still handle JPG uploads better than WebP.
  • Easy Editing: Nearly every photo editor, no matter how basic, supports JPG.
