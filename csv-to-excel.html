About CSV to Excel

CSV (Comma-Separated Values) files are excellent for data portability but lack the powerful features of a full spreadsheet, such as cell styling, filtering, and multi-sheet management. Converting your data to Excel (.xlsx) allows you to perform complex analysis and professional reporting more effectively.

Our converter leverages Client-Side Rendering (CSR). Using the browser's ability to handle binary streams via the SheetJS library, the conversion is performed locally. Your data is never uploaded to a cloud server, ensuring your business intelligence remains confidential.

How to Convert CSV to Excel

  1. Upload CSV: Drag and drop your .csv files or click the box to browse your computer.
  2. Verify: Review the file names in the queue. You can process multiple data sets simultaneously.
  3. Start Process: Click "CONVERT TO EXCEL". The browser will read the text lines and map them to spreadsheet cells.
  4. Download: Your formatted .xlsx files will be generated and saved to your device instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. The conversion logic is written in JavaScript and runs inside your browser. No data leaves your machine during the process.

Does it support different delimiters?

Yes, the tool automatically detects common delimiters like commas, semicolons, and tabs to ensure your data is parsed correctly into columns.

Is there a limit on rows?

While we don't set a hard limit, very large files (hundreds of thousands of rows) may depend on your device's RAM for smooth processing.

Can I convert multiple CSVs?

Yes, you can upload multiple files at once, and the tool will generate a separate Excel workbook for each one.
