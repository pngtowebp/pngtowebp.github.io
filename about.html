About Our Tools

Welcome to PNG to WebP Converter, a suite of high-performance web tools designed for the modern internet. Our mission is to make file conversion fast, free, and—most importantly—private.

Why We Are Different

Unlike traditional online converters that require you to upload your files to a remote server, our tools work using Client-Side Rendering (CSR). We utilize advanced technologies like WebAssembly (Wasm) and HTML5 Canvas to process your files directly in your browser.

Our Core Advantages

  • Privacy: Your files never leave your computer.
  • Speed: No upload or download wait times.
  • Quality: Professional-grade libraries like FFmpeg and UTIF.js ensure high-fidelity results.

Versatility for Every User

Whether you are a photographer dealing with CR2 raw files, a designer needing to flatten a PSD, or a developer optimizing videos for the web, we have a tool built for you. Our technology stack ensures that high-quality conversion is accessible to everyone without the need for expensive software or compromising data security.

We are constantly evolving and adding new formats to our library, maintaining our commitment to a serverless, user-centric experience.
