About MKV to MP4

MKV (Matroska) is a flexible container that can hold many tracks, but it isn't supported by all browsers or mobile devices. MP4 is the universal standard for video playback. Our tool changes the container from MKV to MP4 without re-encoding the video stream whenever possible, ensuring zero quality loss.

This converter uses FFmpeg.wasm. Unlike traditional online converters that require you to upload GBs of data to a server, we run the conversion engine inside your browser using WebAssembly. Your video data never leaves your computer, making it the fastest and most private way to convert large video files.

How to Convert MKV to MP4

  1. Select Video: Drag your .mkv files into the tool or click to browse.
  2. Prepare: The tool loads the WebAssembly core. Wait for the file list to appear.
  3. Convert: Click "CONVERT TO MP4". The browser will remux the audio and video tracks.
  4. Save: Once finished, the .mp4 file will be downloaded to your device instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a quality loss?

No. By default, this tool performs "remuxing," which means it moves the video and audio data from the MKV container to an MP4 container without touching the internal bits.

Is it safe for large files?

Yes. Since the processing is client-side, there are no upload limits. The only limit is your computer's memory and processing power.

Why is it faster than other converters?

Because there is no upload or download time to a remote server. The only time spent is the actual conversion process happening locally.
