About WAV to MP3

WAV files provide the highest possible audio quality because they are uncompressed, but this comes at the cost of very large file sizes. MP3 uses sophisticated psychoacoustic modeling to remove sounds the human ear can't easily hear, drastically reducing file size (often by 90%) while maintaining excellent clarity.

Our tool runs Client-Side. By using the `lamejs` encoder, your browser performs the math required to compress the audio. This ensures your private recordings stay on your computer and never touch a remote server.

How to Convert WAV to MP3

  1. Upload: Drag your .wav files into the upload box or select them from your file manager.
  2. Verify: See your selected files in the preview list. You can batch-process multiple files at once.
  3. Convert: Click "CONVERT TO MP3". The browser will analyze the audio waveform and encode the MP3.
  4. Download: Once finished, the compressed MP3 files will be saved to your device automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much smaller will my file be?

Typically, an MP3 file is about 10% the size of the original WAV file. For example, a 50MB WAV file usually becomes a 5MB MP3.

What bitrate is used?

The tool converts audio at 128kbps, which provides a perfect balance between significant file size reduction and high-quality stereo sound.

Can I convert voice recordings?

Yes. This tool is ideal for converting voice notes, interviews, or podcasts recorded in WAV format to MP3 for easier sharing.

Is it safe to use?

Yes. Since no files are uploaded to our servers, your data is completely secure and private within your own browser session.
