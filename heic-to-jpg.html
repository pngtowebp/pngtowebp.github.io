About HEIC to JPG Converter

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Coding) is the standard photo format for iPhones and modern Apple devices. While it saves space, it often causes compatibility issues when sharing with Windows users or uploading to various websites. Our HEIC to JPG converter solves this problem instantly.

This tool is built using Client-Side Rendering (CSR), which means your private photos are never sent to a server. Your browser handles the entire conversion locally, providing both speed and total data privacy.

How to Convert HEIC to JPG

  1. Select Files: Drag and drop your .heic or .heif files into the upload box, or click to browse your files.
  2. Verify List: Your selected files will appear in a list. You can remove any files you don't want to convert.
  3. Convert: Click the "CONVERT TO JPG" button. The conversion process will begin immediately.
  4. Download: Once processed, the high-quality JPG versions will be saved directly to your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to convert my personal photos here?

Yes. Unlike other converters that upload your images to their servers, our tool processes everything inside your own browser. Your photos never leave your computer or phone.

Why are my JPGs larger than the original HEIC files?

HEIC uses advanced compression that is roughly twice as efficient as JPG. When converting to JPG for compatibility, the file size usually increases to maintain the same level of visual quality.

Can I convert multiple HEIC photos at once?

Absolutely. You can select multiple files or drag entire folders of photos to convert them in a single batch.

Does this work on mobile?

Yes, this tool is fully responsive and works on iPhones, iPads, and Android devices directly through the web browser.
