About PNG to WebP

WebP is a modern image format that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. Using WebP, webmasters and developers can create smaller, richer images that make the web faster. Converting PNG to WebP can reduce file sizes by up to 30% while maintaining the same quality and transparency.

Our tool uses Client-Side Rendering (CSR). This means the actual conversion happens right in your browser. Since no images are sent to a server, your data remains completely private, and the conversion is limited only by your computer's speed.
